## EverShelf for Home Assistant

Integrates your self-hosted EverShelf pantry manager with Home Assistant.

**What you get:**
- **Sensors**: Expiring soon, Expired items, Total items, Shopping count
- **Binary sensors**: Problem indicators for expired/expiring products
- **Todo entity**: Full bidirectional shopping list sync
- **Services**: Add to shopping, mark as used, force refresh
- **Auto-discovery**: Zeroconf/mDNS when avahi-daemon is available
- **5 languages**: English, Italian, German, French, Spanish

**Requirements:**
- EverShelf 1.7.0+ (self-hosted) — [github.com/dadaloop82/EverShelf](https://github.com/dadaloop82/EverShelf)
- Home Assistant 2024.1.0+
